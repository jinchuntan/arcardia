How to scan

1

Allow camera

When prompted, tap Allow. Your camera is used only for tracking and is not stored.

2

Fill the frame

Hold the card steady and let it fill around 30 to 60 percent of the view.

3

Good lighting

Avoid glare. Bright, even lighting helps the card detect faster.

Quick tip

Start with one card. Once it detects consistently, we add more cards and build your collection flow.

Back Start scanning
If detection is slow: move closer, reduce reflections, and keep the card flat to the camera.
